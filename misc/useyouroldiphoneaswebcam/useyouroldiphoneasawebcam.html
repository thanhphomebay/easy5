Use Your Old Iphone As A Webcam

I have a few old iphones laying around picking up dust. When I said old, they are pretty old. I have 4 of them ranging from 3gs to iphone 5. All of them have old iOS versions. So when I looked for the webcam App I like, they all required to upgrade to the latest iOS. As you may know, with dated hardware, you don't want to install the lastest iOS because it will slow down a lot. Further more, even if you want to upgrade, it won't be available for your old iPhone. I found an app that only requires iOS version 4.3 or later. Its name is iphCAM. Notice that it is not a video stream, the image is updated every 1/2 second. Below are steps to follow:

  1. Get the iphCAM app from AppleStore

  2. Run the App

  3. Open your browser with the address that shown in the red circle in the above picture above. In my case it is http://192.168.1.117:8080

    4. Also in order to save your iphone battery life, you need to put the iphone into the airplane mode and turn down the dimness of your iphone's screen.
